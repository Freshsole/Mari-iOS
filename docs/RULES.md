# Detailed Rules – Lízaný Mariáš

## Deck
32 cards, German suits: Červené (Hearts), Zelené (Leaves), Žaludy (Acorns), Kule (Bells).
Ranks: 7, 8, 9, 10, Spodek (J), Svršek (Q), Král (K), Eso (A).

## Dealing
1. P1: 4 cards
2. P2: 4 cards
3. 9th card face-up → Trumf
4. P1: 4 cards
5. P2: 4 cards
6. 15 cards → Talón (cross on trump card)

## Phase 1 (Talón active)
- No suit obligation, no overtrump obligation
- Winner draws first, loser second
- 8 cards in hand always

## Phase 2 (Talón empty)
1. Must follow suit
2. No obligation to overtrump
3. Must play trump if no suit
4. Discard only if no suit and no trump

## Melds (Hlášky)
K+Q same suit on lead turn: 20 pts (40 if trump suit)

## Scoring
Aces & Tens = 10 each, last trick +10, melds added. Total = 90.
